Profile

Hi, my name is Giorgio Morra, I was born in Sicily, the land that gave the world Archimedes' pi and canned tuna in oil. Matching the tuna is out of reach, so I settle for being a passionate engineer and researcher specializing in planning, high-level control, and navigation for exploration vehicles. I work across the full autonomy stack, from sensing and state estimation up to planning and control, and I enjoy building machines that can find their own way through places we haven't explored yet. Alongside the controls and software side, I bring an advanced mechanical engineering background.
